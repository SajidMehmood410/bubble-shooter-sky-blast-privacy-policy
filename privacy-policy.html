Privacy Policy

Developer: Logcatofficial

This privacy policy applies to the mobile game "Bubble Nightfall: Puzzle Pop" developed by Logcatofficial. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, including children, and complying with all relevant privacy laws and regulations such as COPPA, GDPR-K, and Google Play's Families Policy.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our app does not directly collect or store any personal or sensitive user data. However, we use third-party services (such as Google AdMob) which may collect anonymized technical data solely for the purpose of serving safe, appropriate ads and managing consent.

2. Advertising and Consent

We have integrated Google AdMob and the User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK to display ads and manage user consent in accordance with international privacy regulations.

  • We have enabled TagForChildDirectedTreatment and TagForUnderAgeOfConsent in the SDK to treat all users as underage for maximum safety.
  • The app uses Max Ad Content Rating set to “G” to ensure only family-safe ads are shown.
  • All ad sources used are part of Google’s Families Self-Certified Ads SDK Program.
  • We have disabled personalized ads and tracking for children and do not transmit advertising identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID/AAID) for users identified as underage.
  • In-app privacy options allow users to update or withdraw consent at any time.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services that may collect limited, anonymized data:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying family-safe banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads.
  • Google UMP SDK – for handling consent messages in line with GDPR and regional regulations.

These services may process non-personal data such as approximate location (for legal compliance), device type, or in-app interactions. No personally identifiable information is collected from children.

For more information, visit How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

4. Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for a general audience, including children under the age of digital consent. We do not knowingly collect, use, or share personal data from children.

  • Ads are shown with non-personalized targeting.
  • No identifiers such as AAID, IMEI, MAC address, or geolocation are collected from children.
  • We ensure all ads and ad networks comply with Google Play’s Families Policy and COPPA standards.

If a parent or guardian believes that personal data has been collected from a child, they can contact us to request deletion.

5. AI-Generated Assets

Some visual effects or design elements may be AI-generated. These do not process or access any user data.

6. Consent and User Control

Consent forms are presented at app launch for users in regions governed by privacy laws. Users may:

  • Choose between personalized and non-personalized ads (when applicable).
  • Review and update consent settings via in-app privacy options.
  • Withdraw consent at any time using the consent button.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store any personal user data ourselves. Third-party partners handle anonymized data according to their own retention policies. No personal data from children is retained.

8. Access to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is available from the app’s settings menu and the Google Play Store listing.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in regulation, SDK providers, or app functionality. Changes will be published on this page with a new effective date.
